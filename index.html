Controls

  • Q - rotate sword left
  • E - rotate sword right
  • Mouse - move sword

Instructions

Position your sword over an enemy's sword for 1 second to parry. Be sure to do so before the enemy completes its attack. Aim for opposite angles (i.e. make a 'cross' or 'x' with your sword and the enemy sword) for the highest score.
